Hi, I’m Andrew

Hey, I’m Andrew and I work as an Android Software Engineer; currently building delightful features for the Skyscanner Android app. I haven’t always worked as an engineer: a few years ago I decided to make a big change from IT Consulting and I haven’t looked back.

I don’t want to rehash my CV here. For that, you can download it, take a look at my StackOverflow developer story, browse my github1 or try any one of other social links in the header up above.

What makes me Tech

The technology products that excite me the most are those that have the greatest impact on simplifying the lives of the individual. I’m less about staring at your smartphone for hours on end and more about it being a trusted companion that’s reliably there when you need it but won’t betray you when you’re not looking.
